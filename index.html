Welcome to Sintes Telegram Bot

About Sintes

Sintes Telegram Bot (@Sintes_bot) is here to help you efficiently manage your daily tasks. It integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar and makes planning as easy as possible through voice commands and text input.

Features

  • Google Calendar Integration: Easily add, update, or delete events in your Google Calendar.
  • Voice Commands: Use voice commands to quickly add tasks to your schedule.
  • Time Zone Support: The bot can adjust to your time zone automatically based on your location.

How to Get Started

  1. Start a Conversation: Find Sintes Bot on Telegram (@Sintes_bot) and click Start.
  2. Integrate Your Calendar: Use the setup commands to connect Sintes to your Google Calendar.
  3. Start Planning: Use voice commands or text input to add tasks, events, and manage your schedule.
